The Oscillating Resonant Group AxioN (ORGAN) Collaboration is an Australian-led effort developing resonant detectors to search for axion dark matter and related physics. ORGAN builds on Australia’s legacy of precision microwave engineering and low-temperature instrumentation, combining expertise from multiple institutions to push sensitivity in the micro– to milli–electron-volt range.

Our experiments use high-Q resonant cavities in strong magnetic fields to convert axions into detectable photons. We are expanding rapidly, with nodes at the University of Western Australia, Swinburne University of Technology, and other partners.
